Everything caregivers need
Monitor patients, track moods, manage medications, and let Reachy handle the daily companionship.
💬

AI Conversations

Natural voice conversations powered by OpenAI. Reachy remembers preferences, tells jokes, shares stories, and provides emotional support.

🧠

Cognitive Games

Memory quizzes, word games, and interactive stories that adapt to the patient's ability level. Scores tracked over time.

📊

Mood Analytics

Real-time mood tracking with facial emotion detection. Weekly reports show trends so caregivers can spot changes early.

💊

Medication Tracking

Schedule reminders, log doses, and track compliance. Reachy gently reminds patients when it's time for their medication.

⚠️

Safety Alerts

Automatic alerts for falls, wandering, agitation, and anomalous behavior. Incident reports with severity tracking and resolution workflow.

🎮

Teleoperation

Remote control Reachy from anywhere — sliders, keyboard, Xbox controller, or mirror mode that mimics your head movements via webcam.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Portal

Family members can send messages, view care plans, and stay connected with their loved one's daily activities and mood.

📝

Shift Handoffs

Auto-generated shift summaries with alerts, mood data, medication status, and caregiver notes. Nothing falls through the cracks.

🔒

Role-Based Access

Admin, caregiver, and family roles with row-level security. Everyone sees exactly what they need — nothing more.
