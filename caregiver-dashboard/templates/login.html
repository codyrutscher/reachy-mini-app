How it works
Three layers, one system.
1

Reachy talks to the patient

Voice conversations powered by OpenAI. Reachy tells jokes, plays memory games, guides meditation, reminds about medications, and detects mood through facial expressions. Everything is logged.

2

Data flows to the dashboard

Conversation logs, mood journals, cognitive scores, sleep patterns, pain reports, and safety alerts stream into a Supabase Postgres database with row-level security. Nothing is lost.

3

Caregivers see everything

The dashboard shows real-time patient status, mood trends, medication compliance, incident reports, and weekly summaries. Admins can teleoperate Reachy remotely or use mirror mode with their webcam.

Capabilities
What Reachy can do.
🗣️

Natural voice conversations

Real-time speech with OpenAI. Reachy remembers names, favorite topics, and personal stories across sessions. Supports multiple languages.

🧠

Cognitive games

Word recall, trivia, interactive stories, personal quizzes. Difficulty adapts automatically. Scores tracked over weeks.

🎵

Music & singalong

Plays favorites, guides singalongs, creates calm environments with ambient melodies.

🧘

Guided meditation

Breathing exercises, body scans, and gratitude journaling with gentle voice guidance.

⚠️

Safety monitoring

Fall detection, wandering alerts, agitation tracking. Automatic caregiver notifications.

🎮

Remote teleoperation

Control Reachy from the browser with sliders, keyboard, Xbox controller, or mirror mode that tracks your face via webcam and mimics your head movements in real-time.

📊

Weekly reports

Auto-generated summaries: mood distribution, top conversation topics, cognitive trends, medication compliance.

💊

Medication reminders

Scheduled reminders with voice prompts. Dose logging and compliance tracking for caregivers.

🔒

Role-based access

Admin, caregiver, family roles. Row-level security on every table. JWT-scoped patient data.
